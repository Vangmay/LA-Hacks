The pairing creates a complete temporal and logical loop for the literature review. The Cartographer provides the grounding in the past (prior_work), mapping the technical lineage to ensure the review identifies the true origins of the concepts. The Miner provides the forward-looking tension (constructive, skeptical, recent_or_future_work) by synthesizing the current state of the field to identify gaps, critiques, and future directions. This ensures the resulting markdown memory is neither a mere list of old papers nor a collection of baseless future conjectures.
