## Rationale

Given the foundational status of 'Attention is All you Need', a standard literature review would be insufficient. The research must be bifurcated into two distinct trajectories: the 'Architectural Genealogy' (how the Transformer's core components evolved into the BERT/GPT paradigms) and the 'Constraint-Driven Innovation' (how researchers are actively attempting to solve the quadratic complexity and memory bottlenecks inherent in the original design). This allows one squad to focus on structural lineage and another to focus on the current frontier of replacing or augmenting attention.
