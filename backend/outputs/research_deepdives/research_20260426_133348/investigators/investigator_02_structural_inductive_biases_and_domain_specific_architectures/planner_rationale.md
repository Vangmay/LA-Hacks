To effectively ideate novel domain-specific architectures, this roster triangulates between the mathematical 'why' (theory), the empirical 'how much' (ablation), and the strategic 'what's missing' (gaps). The Mechanism-First Theorist identifies the inherited structural assumptions of a bias; the Ablation-to-Theory Translator validates whether specific components actually drive the reported gains; and the Research-Gap Miner synthesizes these perspectives to find underexplored intersections or limitations in existing domain-specific priors.
