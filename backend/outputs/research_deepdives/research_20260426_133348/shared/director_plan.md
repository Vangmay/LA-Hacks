## Rationale

To drive novelty ideation from the Transformer, we focus on breaking its most rigid constraints: the quadratic scaling bottleneck, the lack of intrinsic inductive bias for non-sequential data, and the static nature of its token processing. These directions target the current 'post-Transformer' frontier where State Space Models and dynamic architectures are emerging.
