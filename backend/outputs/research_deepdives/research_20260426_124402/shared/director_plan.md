Instead, I am focusing the budget on two critical technical tension points: the trade-off between the 'Attention' mechanism's quadratic complexity versus efficiency (Investigator 1), and the re-introduction of structural/recurrent priors that were 'dispensed with' but are essential for long-range reasoning (Investigator 2). This maximizes novelty discovery for high-performance, efficient architectures.
